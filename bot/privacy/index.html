Privacy Policy for Sirk Bot

What is stored?

- Songs
- User ID's
- Guild ID's

Why it is stored?

We store data to make your use of Sirk Bot more easy and reliable for the next time of use and to see that everything worked.

Why we need the data we store:

We need the data we store so that Sirk Bot can work and can function properly.
If Sirk Bot does not store data then the commands will not function and it will not work as a whole.

Who gets this data?

We do not share any data with any third-party sources or publicly present your data.

Questions and Concerns:

If you have any questions or concerns you can either email us or you can join our support server.

How to remove your data:

If you wish to remove your data you can either contact us by the means above, or you can remove Sirk Bot from your server. This will thoroughly remove all data.
